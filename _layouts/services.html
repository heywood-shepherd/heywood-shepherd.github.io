Taxation

Tax legislation is complex and specialist advice is essential to both business and individual clients.

Audit Work

Make an appointment   to discuss how we can be of assistance to you - the first hour is free of charge.

We can also help in any of these areas:

Payroll Services

Payroll, like all other aspects of taxation, is becoming increasingly complex.  We can not only advise you on payroll matters, we can also run your payroll for you using the Sage payroll system.

Secretarial Services

We offer all types of company secretarial services ranging from the use of our address as the registered office of the company to completion of the various forms required by Companies House.

Charity Accounting

We have experience in preparing, auditing and independently examining accounts for charities to ensure that they comply with current legislation.

Solicitor's Accounts

We are also specialists in Solicitor's Accounts Rules and can offer accountancy services to firms of solicitors. 

Sage

We offer training, advice, support and are authorised resellers of Sage software.  We also provide book keeping services using Sage software in-house or on client's premises and can provide regular management accounts as required.

Trusts

We can complete Trust tax returns and prepare accounts for most types of Trust.

Estates of the Deceased

We can prepare accounts for the estates of deceased persons, assist with applications for probate, deal with the deceased person's tax liability to the date of death, advise on tax liabilities during the period of administration and give other advice on matters arising during the period of administration of the estate.

Fee Protection Insurance

We offer fee protection insurance to help with costs of investigations by HM Revenue and Customs into our clients tax affairs.

Financial Services

  • Protection planning
  • Retirement planning
  • Investments
  • Private medical insurance and care plans
  • Individual Savings Accounts (ISA's)
